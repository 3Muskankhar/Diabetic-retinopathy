Educational Resources on Diabetic Retinopathy

Stay informed with our comprehensive educational resources about diabetic retinopathy. We provide detailed information on its symptoms, risk factors, and preventive measures, empowering both healthcare professionals and patients alike. Our platform serves as a vital tool for understanding the complexities of diabetic retinopathy, ensuring that everyone is equipped with the knowledge to recognize early signs and take necessary precautions. By fostering awareness and education, we contribute to better patient outcomes and promote proactive healthcare. Explore our resources and enhance your understanding of this critical condition today!

SYMPTOMS

  • Blurred vision
  • Dark spots or floaters
  • Sudden vision loss
  • Difficulty seeing colors

PREVENTION

  • Regular eye check-ups
  • Blood sugar control
  • Healthy diet and exercise
  • Avoid smoking
